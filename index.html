---------- About ----------












ManyMentors is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit founded in 2011 to inspire, encourage, and support underrepresented students’ interest in pursuing science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM) fields, and eventual entrance into the STEM workforce. From middle schoolers to young working professionals, we connect individuals to near-age mentors who can make opportunities in STEM become more visible, valuable & viable.




Our approach is a combination of onsite and online mentoring to coordinate STEM volunteer outreach and facilitate one-on-one mentor/mentee matching & communication so students can gain access to resources, discover local opportunities, & develop professional skills in the STEM fields.

ManyMentors primarily serves middle and high school students in Connecticut, and is currently expanding to other areas in the Northeast. We have student chapters at the University of Connecticut, Cornell University, Yale University, and more! Check out our Mentor Map for more details!
